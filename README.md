# AI Governance Executive Simulations
**Dr. Dipu Patel, DMSc, MPAS, PA-C, ABAIM | ReasonFirst**

Three interactive decision-making simulations for senior leaders in healthcare, pharma, and public health. Each simulation presents a real AI governance crisis and asks participants to make three consequential decisions — under time pressure, with incomplete information.

---

## Simulations

| File | Scenario | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| `meridian_incident.html` | Three AI systems contribute to a patient harm event at a hospital | Hospital C-Suite, Academic Leaders, Public Health Officials, Board Members |
| `safety_signal.html` | A below-threshold AI surveillance signal is ignored. Three patients are harmed. | Pharma and Medtech executives, Regulatory Affairs, Board Members |
| `public_health_cascade.html` | An AI contact tracing system encodes historical inequity. Two people die during the response gap. | Public Health Leadership, State Officials, Policy Makers, Community Leaders |

---

## How to Use

1. Open any `.html` file directly in a browser — Chrome recommended
2. Select your role at the top
3. Read the incident briefing carefully
4. Make three decisions and review your Governance Scorecard
5. Copy your Decision Log from the scorecard for assignment submission

No login. No installation. Internet connection required.
